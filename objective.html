Objectives of This Lesson

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Understand the concept of the Tragedy of the Commons.
  • Analyze how individual actions impact shared resources.
  • Identify real-world examples of resource overuse and depletion.
  • Explore possible solutions for sustainable resource management.
  • Develop a critical perspective on how policies and regulations affect shared resources.

This lesson will help you grasp the importance of balancing **personal interests** with **collective sustainability**.

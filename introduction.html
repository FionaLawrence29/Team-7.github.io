What is the Tragedy of the Commons?

The **Tragedy of the Commons** is a theory that explains how individuals, acting in their self-interest, can deplete shared resources, leading to long-term consequences. It highlights the conflict between personal benefit and collective well-being.

This concept applies to various areas, including environmental issues like overfishing, deforestation, air pollution, and even modern problems such as internet bandwidth congestion and healthcare access.

Understanding this phenomenon is essential for designing policies that balance individual needs with sustainability. In this lesson, we will explore its causes, real-world examples, and potential solutions.
